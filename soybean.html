Soybean Acreage Trend Analysis (2010–2024)

This project analyzes 15 years of soybean acreage trends across Arkansas using USDA CDL data and ArcGIS Pro.

Key Methods

  • Raster extraction and masking
  • Zonal statistics and change detection
  • Mapping spatial patterns and flood impacts
